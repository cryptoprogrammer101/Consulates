    Brazilian Consulate General in Boston, United States

    Boston, MA 2110
    United States
  • Telephone

  • Fax

  • Email

  • boston.itamaraty.gov.br
  • Social media
  • www.facebook.com/BrazilianCGBoston
  • Office hours
  • Monday-Friday: 09:00-15:00
  • Head of mission
  • Mr Frederico Cezar De Araujo, Consul General
  • Consular services
  • No information available
  • Supervising authority
  • Embassy of Brazil in Washington, D.C.

  • Contact details for the Brazilian consulate general in Boston

  • The consulate general of Brazil in Boston is located at 175 Purchase Street, 20 Park Plaza, suite 810 and can be contacted by telephone on (617) 542 4000 as well as by email [email protected]. The consulate general of Brazil in Boston is supervised by the embassy in Washington, D.C..

  • Opening hours

  • The consulate general is open Mondays to Fridays between 09:00 and 15:00. The offices of the consulate general may be closed on U.S. and Brazilian public holidays. Please contact the consulate general to confirm opening hours.

    Due to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, opening hours of the consulate general may be affected.

  • Visa, passport and consular services

  • The consulate general of Brazil in Boston may provide a range of consular services such as visa and passport processing as well as document legalization. Please contact the office directly for information about which consular services they offer.


  • Brazil in the United States

  • In addition to Brazil's consulate general in Boston, Brazil has 16 other representations in the United States. These representations include an embassy in Washington, D.C. and consulates in Atlanta, Chicago, Hartford, Honolulu, Houston, Los Angeles, Memphis, Miami, New Orleans, New York, Norfolk, Phoenix, Salt Lake City, San Diego and San Francisco.

  • United States in Brazil

  • The United States has nine representations in Brazil. These representations include an embassy in Brasilia and consulates in Belo Horizonte, Fortaleza, Manaus, Porto Alegre, Recife, Rio de Janeiro, Salvador and Sao Paulo.

  • Brazil and the United States abroad

  • The Brazilian consulate general is one of 1818 foreign representations in the United States and one of 58 foreign representations in Boston. See more at the United States EmbassyPages. The Brazilian consulate general in Boston is one of 366 Brazilian diplomatic and consular representations abroad. See more at the Brazil EmbassyPages.
