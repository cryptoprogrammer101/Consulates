  • Contact details for the Greek consulate general in Boston

  • . The consulate general of Greece in Boston is supervised by the embassy in Washington, D.C..

  • Opening hours

  • The offices of the consulate general may be closed on U.S. and Greek public holidays. Please contact the consulate general to confirm opening hours.

    Due to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, opening hours of the consulate general may be affected.

  • Visa, passport and consular services

  • The consulate general of Greece in Boston may provide a range of consular services such as visa and passport processing as well as document legalization. Please contact the office directly for information about which consular services they offer.


  • Greece in the United States

  • In addition to Greece's consulate general in Boston, Greece has eight other representations in the United States. These representations include an embassy in Washington, D.C. and consulates in Atlanta, Chicago, Houston, Los Angeles, New York, San Francisco and Tampa.

  • United States in Greece

  • The United States has two representations in Greece. These representations include an embassy in Athens and a consulate general in Thessaloniki.

  • Greece and the United States abroad

  • The Greek consulate general is one of 1818 foreign representations in the United States and one of 58 foreign representations in Boston. See more at the United States EmbassyPages.
