  • Contact details for the South Korean consulate general in Boston

  • . The consulate general of the Republic of Korea in Boston is supervised by the embassy in Washington, D.C..

  • Opening hours

  • The offices of the consulate general may be closed on U.S. and South Korean public holidays. Please contact the consulate general to confirm opening hours.

    Due to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, opening hours of the consulate general may be affected.

  • Visa, passport and consular services

  • The consulate general of the Republic of Korea in Boston may provide a range of consular services such as visa and passport processing as well as document legalization. Please contact the office directly for information about which consular services they offer.


  • The Republic of Korea in the United States

  • In addition to the Republic of Korea's consulate general in Boston, the Republic of Korea has 19 other representations in the United States. These representations include an embassy in Washington, D.C. and consulates in Anchorage, Atlanta, Chicago, Dallas, Detroit, Eugene, Honolulu, Houston, Los Angeles, Miami, New Orleans, New York, Portland, OR, Salt Lake City, San Francisco, Seattle, St. Louis and Tucson.

  • United States in the Republic of Korea

  • The United States has two representations in the Republic of Korea. These representations include an embassy in Seoul and a consulate in Busan.

  • The Republic of Korea and the United States abroad

  • The South Korean consulate general is one of 1818 foreign representations in the United States and one of 58 foreign representations in Boston. See more at the United States EmbassyPages. The South Korean consulate general in Boston is one of 302 South Korean diplomatic and consular representations abroad. See more at the Republic of Korea EmbassyPages.
