  • Embassy of Belgium in Washington, D.C.

  • Contact details for the Belgian consulate in Boston

  • The consulate of Belgium in Boston can be contacted by telephone on 857-259-2070 as well as by email [email protected]. The consulate of Belgium in Boston is supervised by the embassy in Washington, D.C..

  • Opening hours

  • The consulate is open weekdays by appointment. The offices of the consulate may be closed on U.S. and Belgian public holidays. Please contact the consulate to confirm opening hours.

    Due to the ongoing Covid-19 pandemic, opening hours of the consulate may be affected.

  • Visa, passport and consular services

  • The consulate offers limited consular services and does not accept visa applications.

    NO visa processing

  • Belgium in the United States

  • In addition to Belgium's consulate in Boston, Belgium has 33 other representations in the United States. These representations include an embassy in Washington, D.C. and consulates in Atlanta, Austin, Baltimore, Chicago, Cincinnati, Columbia, Dallas, Denver, Detroit, Greenwich, Honolulu, Houston, Jacksonville, Kansas City, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Louisville, Miami, Milwaukee, Moline, New Orleans, New York, Norfolk, Philadelphia, Phoenix, Pittsburgh, Portland, OR, Raleigh, Salt Lake City, San Francisco, St. Louis and St. Paul.

  • United States in Belgium

  • The United States maintains an embassy in Brussels.

  • Belgium and the United States abroad

  • The Belgian consulate is one of 1818 foreign representations in the United States and one of 58 foreign representations in Boston. See more at the United States EmbassyPages. The Belgian consulate in Boston is one of 436 Belgian diplomatic and consular representations abroad. See more at the Belgium EmbassyPages.
