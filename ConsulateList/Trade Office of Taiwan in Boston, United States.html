  • The offices of the trade office may be closed on U.S. and Taiwanese public holidays. Please contact the trade office to confirm opening hours.

  • Visa, passport and consular services

  • The trade office of Taiwan in Boston may provide a range of consular services such as visa and passport processing as well as document legalization. Please contact the office directly for information about which consular services they offer.


  • Taiwan in the United States

  • In addition to Taiwan's trade office in Boston, Taiwan has ten other representations in the United States. These representations include representative offices in Atlanta, Chicago, Honolulu, Houston, Los Angeles, Miami, New York, San Francisco, Seattle and Washington, D.C..

  • United States in Taiwan

  • The United States has two representations in Taiwan. These representations include representative offices in Kaohsiung and Taipei.

  • Taiwan and the United States abroad

  • The Taiwanese trade office is one of 1818 foreign representations in the United States and one of 58 foreign representations in Boston. See more at the United States EmbassyPages. The Taiwanese trade office in Boston is one of 110 Taiwanese diplomatic and consular representations abroad. See more at the Taiwan EmbassyPages.
